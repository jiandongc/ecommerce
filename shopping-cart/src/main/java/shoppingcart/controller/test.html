Protecting your Privacy

At PrettyLittleThing, we’ve got your back when it comes to your style and those all-important personal details. Our customers are super important to us, which means protecting our customers by keeping their personal data and information secure at all costs is a main priority. We know the world of data security can be tricky (and a little zzzz), but we want you to be fully clued up on everything you need to know when it comes to your personal information and how it’s used. Consider this your guide to all things privacy related.

The Lowdown On Information We Gather When Shopping With Us
How We Get Your Info

Most of the information you, the customer, provide us with happens when you’re engaging with us and our brand. This could be from anything from browsing the site, to purchasing and registering for an account.

When creating and registering an account, you may provide us with personal information including your name, email address, delivery address, phone number, credit card details, date of birth and other general marketing preferences.

We may also receive information from you via you entering one of our competitions, participating in any feedback questionnaires around your shopping experience with us, or from any communication you may have with our customer services team.

For more information please see our full privacy policy

Dressy Tops

After ditching the dresses, we are filling our wardrobes up with this seasons must-have dressy tops for women. Since jeans and a nice top combo has become our new weekend go-to, we just had to create a category for all going out dressy tops. From figure-hugging thong bodysuits; that can be paired with any bottoms to statement dressy blouses to take your simple pair of denims to the next level. Looking for that new outfit saviour? Invest in minimalistic dressy t shirts that you can get creative with and style to fit any occasion. Want to inject some sass to your wardrobe? Go for a dressy crop top with statement sleeves and finish off the look with some vinyl skinnies. From plunging necklines to midriff-baring crops, whatever you’re looking for we’ve got the perfect main range, petite, shape and plus size dressy tops for you.
